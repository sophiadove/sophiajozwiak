About Me

I’m a multimedia creator with experience in social media management, digital communications, graphic design and music production. I’m currently the Digital Content & Communities Assistant at WDET, Detroit’s NPR Station, where I manage nine station-related social media accounts and publish a weekly arts and culture events guide. Now based in Houston, TX, I graduated from Michigan State University’s Residential College in the Arts and Humanities (RCAH) with a bachelor’s degree in Arts and Humanities and a minor in Entrepreneurship & Innovation. If you like what you see, reach out and let’s make something together!
